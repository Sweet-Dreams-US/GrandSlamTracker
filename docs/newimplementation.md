4. Sustaining Fee Mechanics
What it does: Ensures we never earn less than last year's average after baseline reset
Formula:
Sustaining Fee = Last Year Avg Monthly Fee - (New Foundation ÷ 12)
Rules:

Only kicks in Year 2+
Can only increase, never decrease
Recalculated at each reset

Worked example: Monster Remodeling Year 1 → Year 2
5. Baseline Reset Mechanics
