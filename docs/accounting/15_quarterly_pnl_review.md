## 1. Purpose

To conduct a deep-dive review of the business every quarter — aggregating three months of P&L data, analyzing trends, evaluating the 35/65 split effectiveness, assessing tax planning progress, and making strategic decisions about pricing, spending, and growth. This is Sweet Dreams' **quarterly board meeting with itself.**

While monthly P&L statements (Document 09) track what happened, this quarterly review asks: **"Is our strategy working? What should we change?"**

## 6. Strategic Review Questions

Answer these every quarter:

### Revenue & Growth
1. Which revenue stream grew the most this quarter? Why?
2. Which stream declined or stalled? What's the root cause?
3. Are we acquiring new clients at a sustainable rate?
4. What's our average project size trending? Up, down, or flat?
5. Is beat sales volume growing enough to justify the platform costs?

### Operations & Efficiency
6. Are we spending too much time on low-revenue activities?
7. Is the 35/65 split still the right ratio?
8. Do we need to hire (W-2) or bring on more contractors?
9. Are there processes that should be automated or systematized?
10. Is our equipment adequate or holding us back?

### Risk & Compliance
11. Are all contractor W-9s current?
12. Are there any outstanding invoices over 60 days?
13. Is our general liability insurance adequate for our current revenue level?
14. Are we approaching economic nexus in any state for sales tax?
15. Are estimated tax payments on track to avoid penalties?
