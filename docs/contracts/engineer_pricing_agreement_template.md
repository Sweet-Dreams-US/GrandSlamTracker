**Current Media Service Pricing:**

| Service | Price | Includes |

**4\. MEDIA PRODUCTION COMPENSATION**
**There are two compensation tiers for media work, depending on the Engineer's role in the project:**
**A.  Sales Commission (Upselling Only)**
**When the Engineer upsells any media service to a studio client (music videos, shorts, photo shoots, etc.) but is not the person producing or shooting the content, the Engineer receives a 15% sales commission on the total sale.**

| Media Sold | Total Sale | Engineer Earns (15%) |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Music Video -- Basic** | **$500** | **$75** |
| **Music Video -- Standard** | **$1,500** | **$225** |
| **Music Video -- Premium** | **$3,000** | **$450** |
| **Short-Form Content** | **$100** | **$15** |

**B.  Production Work (Engineer Produces the Content)**
**When the Engineer is the one producing or shooting media content, the revenue split is:**

| Engineer Earns | Studio Retains |
| :---- | :---- |
| **65%** | **35%** |

**Example Production Earnings:**

| Project | Total Sale | Engineer Earns (65%) |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Music Video -- Basic** | **$500** | **$325** |
| **Music Video -- Standard** | **$1,500** | **$975** |
| **Music Video -- Premium** | **$3,000** | **$1,950** |
| **Short-Form Content** | **$100** | **$65** |

**5\. INITIAL RAMP-UP PERIOD**
**During the initial period of this agreement, all music video and intensive media production projects will be handled by the Sweet Dreams US production team. This allows the Studio to maintain its established quality standard while evaluating the Engineer's production capabilities over time.**
**During this period, the Engineer's media compensation will be limited to the 15% sales commission outlined in Section 4A. As the Engineer demonstrates the ability to meet the Studio's quality standards, production work (and the 65/35 split) may be made available at the Studio's discretion.**

**6\. EXIT & TERMINATION**
**Either party may terminate this agreement at any time, for any reason, with a minimum of 30 days' written notice.**
**The Studio reserves the right to end this agreement if, at its sole discretion, the working relationship is not the right fit. This includes but is not limited to concerns around quality of work, professionalism, client experience, reliability, or alignment with the Studio's standards and brand.**
**Upon termination, the Engineer will be compensated for all completed sessions and any earned sales commissions up through the date of termination. No further compensation will be owed by either party after that date.**
