## Unique Features Required

### 1. Expense Budget Tracking
MC Racing needs detailed expense tracking built into their contract/financials view because:
- They operate a facility with fixed overhead (rent, utilities, equipment)
- Need to model profitability alongside revenue growth
- Currently operating at a loss - need to track path to profitability

### 2. Revenue Streams
Different from remodeling - their revenue comes from:
- Drop-in Sessions (primary)
- Private Parties
- Memberships (recurring)
- League Nights
- Merchandise

### 3. Transaction/Accounting System
- Manual income/expense entry
- Account categorization (matches revenue streams and expense categories)
- Running cash flow tracking

### 4. Growth Target Options
- **Percentage-based**: 50%, 100%, 200%, 300%, 400%, 500%
- **Revenue target**: Input specific annual revenue goal (e.g., $150,000/yr)
- System auto-calculates required growth % from revenue target

## Seasonal Factors
Sim racing has specific seasonal patterns:
- Peak: Winter months (Nov-Feb) - people stay indoors
- Dip: Summer months (Jun-Aug) - outdoor activities compete
- Steady: Spring/Fall shoulder seasons
