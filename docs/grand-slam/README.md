## Minimum Thresholds

| Our Stage | Min Monthly Fee | Action if Below |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Early (Now) | $1,000/month | Add minimum fee |
| Year 2+ | $2,000/month | Renegotiate or don't renew |
| Mature | $3,000/month | Only ideal clients |

## Quick Viability Check

*Can this client hit our $1,000/month minimum?*

| Baseline | Growth Needed | Difficulty |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| $3,000/mo | 300%+ | Very difficult |
| $4,000/mo | 200%+ | Difficult |
| $5,000/mo | 150%+ | Stretch |
| $6,000/mo | 100%+ | Achievable |
| $8,000/mo | 75%+ | Reasonable |
| $10,000/mo | 50%+ | Comfortable |
| $15,000/mo | 35%+ | Easy |
| $20,000/mo | 25%+ | Very easy |

**Rule of Thumb:** If `Baseline × 0.10 > $1,000`, likely viable.

## Three Client Tiers

### Tier 1: IDEAL
- **Baseline:** $20k-100k+/month
- **Growth:** 50-200%+
- **Projected Fee:** $3,000-10,000+/month
- **Strategy:** Full effort, long-term partnership

### Tier 2: DEVELOPMENT
- **Baseline:** $8k-20k/month
- **Growth:** 50-100%
- **Projected Fee:** $1,000-3,000/month
- **Strategy:** Standard + minimum fee, evaluate at renewal

### Tier 3: INCUBATOR
- **Baseline:** Under $8k/month
- **Growth:** 100%+ required
- **Projected Fee:** Under $1,000 without minimums
- **Strategy:** Only if strategic value, require minimums

## Key Formulas

| Formula | Calculation |
|---------|-------------|
| **Foundation Fee** | `Annual Baseline × Category Rate` |
| **Growth Fee** | `Σ (Uplift in Tier × Tier Rate)` |
| **Sustaining Fee** | `Last Year Avg Monthly - (New Foundation ÷ 12)` |
| **New Baseline** | `Old Baseline × Industry Factor × Retention Factor` |
