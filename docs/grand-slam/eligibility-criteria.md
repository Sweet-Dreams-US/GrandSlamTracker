# Eligibility Criteria

## Client Qualification Framework

Use this document to determine if a prospective client qualifies for Grand Slam before committing resources.

---

## The 6-Point Decision Matrix

Score each question. Total determines client tier.

| # | Question | Yes (+1) | No (-1) | Score |
|---|----------|----------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Baseline > $10k/month? | +1 | -1 | ______ |
| 2 | Growth potential > 50%? | +1 | -1 | ______ |
| 3 | Projected Year 1 fee > $1,000/month? | +1 | -1 | ______ |
| 4 | Owner is coachable/hungry? | +1 | -1 | ______ |
| 5 | We have capacity? | +1 | -1 | ______ |
| 6 | Strategic value? (case study, referrals) | +1 | 0 | ______ |
| | **TOTAL** | | | ______ |

### Scoring Guide

| Score | Classification | Action |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| **5-6** | Ideal Client | Standard terms |
| **3-4** | Development Client | Add minimum fee |
| **1-2** | Incubator Client | Require retainer/special terms |
| **0 or below** | Pass | Decline politely |

## Client Tier Definitions

### Tier 1: IDEAL

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Baseline | $20k-100k+/month |
| Growth Potential | 50-200%+ |
| Projected Monthly Fee | $3,000-10,000+/month |
| Strategy | Full effort, long-term partnership |
| Contract Terms | Standard Grand Slam |

**Typical Examples:**
- Monster Remodeling (established contractor)
- Regional home service companies
- Multi-location medical practices

**Why They Work:**
- High baseline means meaningful foundation fee
- Growth potential means significant upside
- Can absorb market fluctuations
- Serious about growth

---

### Tier 2: DEVELOPMENT

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Baseline | $8k-20k/month |
| Growth Potential | 50-100% |
| Projected Monthly Fee | $1,000-3,000/month |
| Strategy | Standard + minimum fee, evaluate at renewal |
| Contract Modification | Add $500-750/month minimum |

**Typical Examples:**
- Fort Wayne Female Remodeler
- Growing local contractors
- Established but small businesses

**Why They Need Minimums:**
- Baseline alone doesn't guarantee $1k/month
- Growth could be slower than projected
- Minimum protects our floor

---

### Tier 3: INCUBATOR

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Baseline | Under $8k/month |
| Growth Potential | 100%+ required |
| Projected Monthly Fee | Under $1,000 without minimums |
| Strategy | Only if strategic value, require minimums |

**Contract Options:**
- $500/month minimum fee
- $750/month retainer (hybrid model)
- 90-day trial period

**When to Consider:**
- Hidden gem in scarce market
- Strong referral potential
- Case study opportunity
- Owner shows exceptional drive

## The Hidden Gem Framework

### Definition
A **Hidden Gem** is an undervalued business in a scarce market with execution problems (not demand problems).

The key insight: They don't need more customers to exist—they just need to capture the ones already looking.

### Hidden Gem Signals (Good) ✅

| Signal | Why It's Promising |
|--------|-------------------|
| Scarce/niche market | Low competition for attention |
| Owner knows their craft | Just bad at business/marketing |
| Terrible current marketing | Easy wins available |
| No systems in place | Basics will create immediate lift |
| 1-3 years operating | Survived = something works |
| Good reviews (but few) | Product is good, just low volume |
| Frustrated but motivated | Will actually implement changes |

### Red Flags (Bad) ❌

| Signal | Why It's Problematic |
|--------|---------------------|
| Crowded market | Can't differentiate |
| Blames others for problems | Won't implement recommendations |
| Product/service problems | Marketing can't fix bad product |
| Plateaued 5+ years | Hit a ceiling for a reason |
| Bad reviews | Core business issue |
| Wants to "stay small" | No growth ambition |
| Location problem | We can't fix geography |

## Scarce Market Checklist

Before taking a sub-$10k baseline client, verify market scarcity.

| Question | Good Answer | Bad Answer |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| Competitors within 30 miles? | 0-3 | 10+ |
| Is demand growing in this market? | Yes | No / Declining |
| Average ticket size? | $50+ | Under $20 |
| Can they scale without major capital? | Yes | No |
| Why aren't they growing? | "Don't know marketing" | "No demand" |
| Customer experience quality? | Great, good reviews | Complaints |

**Requirement:** 5 of 6 "Good Answers" to proceed with incubator client.

## Industry Considerations

### High-Potential Industries
- **Remodeling/Construction** - High tickets, strong demand
- **Medical/Healthcare** - Stable, growing market
- **Home Services** - Essential, recurring
- **Legal** - High value per client

### Challenging Industries (Require Minimums)
- **Restaurants** - Thin margins, high failure rate
- **Retail** - Commoditized, Amazon competition
- **Entertainment** - Seasonal, discretionary spending

### Industries to Avoid
- MLM/Network Marketing
- Heavily regulated (without compliance expertise)
- Adult content
- Get-rich-quick schemes
- Anything we can't verify revenue

## Pre-Qualification Questions

### Discovery Call Script

| Question | Good Answer | Bad Answer |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| "What's your current monthly revenue?" | Specific number, verified | Vague, evasive |
| "What's your goal in the next 12 months?" | 2x, 3x with specifics | "Just more" |
| "What have you tried so far?" | Lists attempts, learned | "Nothing works" |
| "Are you ready to invest time?" | "Yes, I'm all in" | "Depends" |
| "Can you share your Google Analytics?" | "Sure, here's access" | "What's that?" |
| "Who's your competition?" | Knows names, weaknesses | "No one / Everyone" |
| "Why do customers choose you?" | Clear value prop | "I don't know" |

### Red Flag Phrases
- "I've tried everything"
- "Marketing doesn't work for my business"
- "I just need more leads"
- "My last 3 agencies failed"
- "I don't have time to be involved"

## Trial Period Structure

### When to Offer
- Incubator clients with high potential
- Development clients with uncertain projections
- Any client where we're uncertain but interested

### Trial Terms
| Element | Standard Trial |
|---------|---------------|
| Duration | 90 days |
| Fee Structure | $500-750/month flat |
| Deliverables | Reduced scope, priority platform only |
| Review Point | Day 60 |
| Conversion Criteria | 30%+ growth trajectory, good communication |

### Conversion Decision Matrix

| 90-Day Result | Action |
|---------------|--------|
| 30%+ growth, great relationship | Convert to full Grand Slam |
| 20-30% growth, good relationship | Convert with minimum fee |
| <20% growth OR difficult relationship | Part ways professionally |

## Final Checklist Before Signing

### Must-Haves ✅
- [ ] Verified baseline revenue (bank statements, Stripe, or analytics)
- [ ] Decision Matrix score of 1 or higher
- [ ] Owner responsive within 24 hours during discovery
- [ ] Clear growth potential identified
- [ ] No red flag phrases in discovery
- [ ] Industry we can serve well

### Nice-to-Haves
- [ ] Existing Google Analytics with 6+ months data
- [ ] Current marketing they're doing (shows investment mindset)
- [ ] Referral from existing client
- [ ] Case study potential

### Deal-Breakers ❌
- [ ] Can't verify revenue
- [ ] Owner unresponsive during discovery
- [ ] Industry we can't serve
- [ ] Unrealistic expectations that can't be managed
- [ ] Decision Matrix score below 1
